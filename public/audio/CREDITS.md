# Built-in background music — provenance

Every track bundled here is **CC0 1.0 (public domain)**: free for commercial use,
no attribution required. We keep this file anyway as a provenance trail. Verified
per-track at the source (a license facet on an aggregator is not enough).

CC0 waives copyright only — we avoid tracks with sampled hooks or recognizable
voices. When swapping/adding a track, confirm CC0 at the source and update this file.

## Notes
- Users can also upload their own audio in the studio (processed locally in the
  browser; never stored). That path carries a rights disclaimer — uploaded
  commercial music can get a Reel muted/blocked by Content ID.
- Excluded by policy: Pixabay and YouTube Audio Library *files* (their terms
  forbid standalone redistribution — fine to use, not to re-host in a repo) and
  CC-BY tracks (attribution would be stripped by users).
